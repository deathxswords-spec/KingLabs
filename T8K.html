Experiment Subject Profile: The Eighth King

Code Name: T8K

Primary Physician: Dr. Chelsea Talisman

Physical Attributes

  • Height: 8'0"
  • Weight: Variable (bio-armor dependent)
  • Size Class: Large
  • Build: Muscular, adaptable

Behavior

  • Temperament: Aggressive under stress, calculated otherwise
  • Response to Containment: Resistant but cooperative with Dr. Talisman
  • Intelligence Level: Above average; problem-solving observed
  • Observations: Talks to himself in containment
  • Threat Rating: High

Abilities

  • Fusion
  • Complex Transmutation
  • Bio-Armor
  • Enhanced Strength
  • Enhanced Speed
  • Enhanced Smell

Containment Information

  • Containment Level: Level 4 — High Security
  • Chamber Specs: Reinforced vault, thermal regulation, kinetic dampeners, Iron Lining
  • Nervous System Module: Model H9v2
  • Observation: Continuous monitoring required

Research Notes

While bio-armor is heat and scratch resistant, prolonged usage leads to reduced endurance due to the absence of sweat glands. Careful observation is required when subject displays stress responses, as violent outbursts have been documented. T8K from camera angles appears to be talking to himself, this behavoir has been studied as he only talks facing one camera, our urgent efforts to immprove his mental health has been challenger. His social Needs have become more concerning as he suggest we release the other TK experiments, we are still debating this decision as we know the importance of mental health on the brain and we don't want the master to be affected by this.
